  • Dominant color scheme: bright primary colors — red, blue, yellow, black-white checkered. Muted terracotta/cream palette essentially absent

  • Gap verdict: Massive demand, zero competition at your aesthetic level. Likely fastest first-sale listing.

Your Trend Pulse Library
These prompts are designed to run on a schedule. Each one pulls forward intelligence — not what is popular now, but what will peak in 6–8 weeks when your suite is designed, listed, and indexed.
Monthly · Last Sunday of Every Month
Forward Trend Scan
"Run a forward trend scan for my Etsy party printables shop. Look 60–90 days ahead: what cultural moments, seasonal peaks, TV or movie releases, sporting events, or viral social trends will create demand for party themes I could design now? For each opportunity: what is the moment, when does search peak, is there a licensing risk, what does current Etsy supply look like? Prioritize gaps where elevated design is missing and there is no licensing issue."
Set a recurring calendar reminder: last Sunday of every month.
Quarterly · Start of Each Season
Seasonal Deep Dive
"Run a seasonal deep dive for [Fall / Holiday / Spring / Summer]. What birthday and party themes peak in this season, what does Pinterest and Etsy search volume look like, what is the ideal design and list date to catch peak traffic, and what themes are underserved this season specifically? Give me a priority-ordered list of what to design first."
Run: late August (fall), late October (holiday), late January (spring), late April (summer).
As Needed · When Something Goes Viral
Rapid Trend Validation
"[Name of trend] is gaining momentum right now. Can I build a party printable suite around it? Check: licensing risk, Etsy market right now (how many listings, what quality), what my design differentiation would be, and if I start designing today how long do I have before the market saturates? Give me a go or no-go recommendation with reasoning."
Use any time something catches your eye — a show, a song, a cultural moment, a meme with party potential.
Monthly · End of Every Month
Data to Action
"Here are my numbers for this month: [Pinterest impressions, top pins by click, Etsy views per listing, favorites, conversions, Amazon affiliate clicks and commission]. Tell me what this data means, which suite is showing the most signal, what my Pinterest content mix should shift toward, and what I should build or change next month based purely on what the numbers say."
